Yonsei University

Yonsei University is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in South Korea. It is located in Seoul and is known for its beautiful campus, rigorous academic programs, and international focus.

Seoul National University

Seoul National University (SNU) is the most prestigious university in South Korea. It is located in the capital city, Seoul, and offers a wide range of academic disciplines.

KAIST

KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology) is a leading research university in Korea, specializing in science and technology. It has a global reputation for innovation and research excellence.

POSTECH

POSTECH (Pohang University of Science and Technology) is one of Korea’s leading private research universities, known for its focus on science and engineering.

Korea University

Korea University is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Korea, known for its strong humanities and business programs. It is located in Seoul.

Sungkyunkwan University

Sungkyunkwan University is a private university in Seoul. It is one of the oldest educational institutions in Korea, with a long history dating back to the Joseon dynasty.

Hanyang University

Hanyang University is known for its strong engineering and business programs. It is located in Seoul and has a large student population.

Ewha Womans University

Ewha Womans University is one of the largest women’s universities in the world. It is located in Seoul and has a strong academic reputation, particularly in the humanities and social sciences.
